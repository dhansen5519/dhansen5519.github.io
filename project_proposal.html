Vision Statement

Motivation

Unity is one of the most widely-used game engines in the industry, and learning how to use it effectively will help prepare me for jobs where it is used. A 2D platformer, with its focus on movement mechanics, physics, and level design, provides an ideal starting point for understanding Unity's capabilities.
